Think of it as something like "Kinect Dancepad".

To begin, accept the camera request so the game can use the camera. Press START!, then stand back until the camera sees your upper body and enough that your arms can reach all buttons on the screen.

To trigger the buttons, wait for them to light up blue. If you hit/swat them correctly, they will turn green. If you miss, they turn red. Don't worry though, I set the buttons to extra sensitive. Best played under bright conditions.

I appologize, I made no in-game restart. You can reload the browser though :)
